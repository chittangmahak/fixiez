About FIXIEZ

We're revolutionizing the device repair industry by creating a trusted ecosystem for both repair professionals and DIY enthusiasts. Our mission is to make quality repairs accessible, affordable, and sustainable.

Our Mission

Our Mission & Vision

At FIXIEZ, we believe that repairing your electronics should be straightforward and sustainable. We are more than just a parts supplier; we are a community-focused platform providing the backbone for the repair industry.

From OEM-grade screens and durable batteries to professional-grade tools and detailed tutorials, FIXIEZ equips you with everything needed to breathe new life into your devices, reducing e-waste and saving you money.

Our vision is to create a world where device repair is the first choice, not the last resort - empowering both individuals and businesses with the tools, knowledge, and confidence to fix anything.

The Problem We Solve

Transforming the chaotic repair industry into a trusted ecosystem

The Problem

  • Unorganized repair market with no standard pricing
  • Lack of trust in repair quality and parts authenticity
  • Inconvenient service experiences and long repair times
  • Uncertainty about repair costs and timelines
  • Difficulty finding reliable parts and repair guides

Our Solution

  • Standardized pricing and service quality across all locations
  • Verified parts with quality grading and warranty
  • Digital platform for booking, tracking, and support
  • Transparent cost estimates and repair timelines
  • Comprehensive knowledge base and repair guides
