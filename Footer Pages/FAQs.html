What is Fixiez?
Fixiez is India's trusted platform for buying premium mobile spare parts, accessories, and providing mobile repairs. We offer genuine parts for all major brands with a 6-month warranty on all products. Our repair services are handled by certified technicians with years of experience.
Do you provide returns or replacements?
Yes, Fixiez offers returns and replacements based on our Return Policy. You can return products within 7 days of delivery if they are defective, damaged, or incorrect. Make sure to record an unboxing video for proof. Replacement requests are processed within 24 hours of receiving the returned item.
How long does shipping take?
Orders are usually delivered in 2–5 business days, depending on your location. For remote areas, delivery may take up to 7 business days. We provide real-time tracking for all orders so you can monitor your shipment's progress.
Do you offer COD?
Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for selected products and locations. The maximum COD order value is ₹5,000. For orders above this amount, prepayment is required. Please note that COD orders may take 1-2 extra days for processing.
What if I receive a damaged product?
You must provide a clear unboxing video showing the damage. After verification, we provide replacement or refund as per policy. Please report damaged products within 24 hours of delivery. Our support team will guide you through the replacement process.
How can I contact Fixiez support?
You can contact us via email at support@fixiez.com, WhatsApp support (+91-9876543210), or the Contact page available on our website. Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM. For urgent repair queries, we offer 24/7 chat support.
Are your spare parts genuine?
Yes, all our spare parts are 100% genuine and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. Each part comes with a 6-month warranty and quality certification. We never compromise on quality and ensure compatibility with your device.
Do you provide repair services at home?
Yes, we offer doorstep repair services in 15+ major cities across India. Our certified technicians will visit your location at a scheduled time. For other locations, you can ship your device to our service center with free pickup and drop-off service.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. All transactions are secured with 128-bit SSL encryption to ensure your financial data remains safe.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track your order by logging into your Fixiez account and visiting the 'My Orders' section. Our tracking system provides real-time updates on your shipment's location.
