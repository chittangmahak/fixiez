Overview

At FIXIEZ, we are committed to offering high-quality mobile parts and accessories. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we make the return process simple and smooth.

Important: Please record an unboxing video of all products to ensure a smooth return process in case of damaged or incorrect items.

Return Eligibility

  • Product must be unused and in original packaging with all accessories.
  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Screen assemblies must have no scratches, no flex damage, and seal must be intact.
  • Batteries must show 0 charge cycles and original packaging intact.
  • Refunds processed only after inspection at our service center.
  • Software-related issues are not eligible for returns unless proven defective.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping unless the part arrives damaged or incorrect.

Warranty

Lifetime warranty products are eligible for replacement only if the defect is non-physical.

  • Screen assemblies: 6 months warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Batteries: 12 months warranty with capacity guarantee
  • Charging ports: 3 months warranty
  • Cameras: 6 months warranty

Note: Warranty does not cover physical damage, liquid damage, or unauthorized repairs.

Refund Process

  • Refunds issued to original payment method within 3-7 business days after approval.
  • Store credit option available for instant refunds to your FIXIEZ account.
  • Cash on Delivery orders refunded via bank transfer only.
  • Partial refunds may apply for opened packages or missing accessories.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Software downloads and digital products
  • Opened consumables like adhesives, thermal paste
  • Custom ordered or special request items
  • Products marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items damaged due to improper installation

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log into your FIXIEZ account and go to "Order History"
  2. Select the item you wish to return and click "Return Item"
  3. Select the reason for return from the dropdown menu
  4. Print the return label (if applicable)
  5. Pack the item securely in original packaging
  6. Drop off at the designated shipping partner
  7. Track your return status in your account
